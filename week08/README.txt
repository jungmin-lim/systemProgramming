ping pong - by jungmin

USAGE: ./hw6 symbol ball_start_col ball_start_row top_row bot_row l_edge r_edge

game ends when one players hits 15 points

player1: 'a' to move upward, 's' to move downward
player2: 'l' to move upward, 'k' to move downward

'q' to slowdown x coordinate
'w' to slowdown y coordinate
'e' to speedup x coordinate
'r' to speedup y coordinate
